About Me

Hi, I'm Ekaterina Azarova, an award-winning nail artist and certified educator based in Halifax, Canada. With years of experience in the beauty industry, I've developed my signature "One Bit Technique" that has transformed how nail technicians approach Russian manicure.

My mission is to share professional techniques that help nail artists achieve premium-quality results efficiently. Through my online courses, you'll learn methods that I use daily in my practice — techniques that allow you to work confidently with any cuticle type and deliver exceptional results to your clients.
