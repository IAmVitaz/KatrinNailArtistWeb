Types of Manicure Bits

Bits are not "magic shapes for every skin type."
They are a professional working tool.

The quality of a manicure is determined not by the number of bits used, but by a clear understanding of how and why each bit is applied.

This guide provides structured, professional information on bits for e-file and combined manicure:

  • existing bit shapes
  • the meaning of the colored band on bits
  • where each bit performs most effectively

This guide is designed for nail technicians who want to understand their tools and use them correctly and safely in their work.
