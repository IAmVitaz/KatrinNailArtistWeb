About This Course

This comprehensive online program teaches you a revolutionary single-bit technique for professional manicures that works universally on all skin types. Say goodbye to switching between multiple drill bits — master one technique that handles everything.

The course is based on techniques I use daily in my practice, refined through years of experience working with clients of all cuticle types.
