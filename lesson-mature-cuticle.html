Lesson: Working With a Mature Cuticle

Master safe and gentle techniques for handling thin, dry, and sensitive skin during manicures

About This Lesson

About This Lesson

This lesson focuses on techniques for handling delicate skin during manicures. The content addresses thin, dry, and sensitive skin that requires a particularly careful and controlled approach. You'll learn how to work safely and gently without causing skin trauma.

The lesson demonstrates how to adapt your hand movements, pressure, and tool positioning for mature skin needs. You'll see a complete technique walkthrough from cuticle preparation through e-file work using my signature "One Bit Technique," achieving natural, aesthetic results while prioritizing client safety.

In this lesson, you'll learn:

Safe and gentle methods

  • Adapted techniques for delicate, thin skin
  • Client safety and comfort prioritized

Adapted pressure techniques

  • Hand movements adjusted for sensitive skin
  • Tool positioning for mature cuticles

One Bit Technique application

  • Complete walkthrough from cuticle prep to e-file work
  • Achieving natural, aesthetic results safely

Note: This lesson is intended for nail technicians who already have experience with e-file manicures. If you're a beginner, I recommend starting with the Russian Manicure: 1 Bit Technique course instead.
